Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

The data given are statewide summaries and not measurements on the individual level. Correlations computed using group means are sometimes referred to as ecological correlations; they are often stronger than correlations that would be computed at the individual level. In this case, we would expect data on income, poverty, age, obesity, etc., to have a large scatter around the averages resulting in much smaller correlations at the individual level.

Spearmans' rank correlation coefficient was computed using the JavaScript module jStat: The JavaScript Statistical Library
